Longitudinal Approach

Observe the dynamics of apps over time across multiple levels

Temporal Analysis: Visual Design

  • What is the state of adopting certain design patterns?
  • Do new design concepts get adopted immediately by developers or at slow pace?
  • Are there any factors that may have contributed into a sudden increase or decrease in adoption?

Temporal Analysis: Accessibility

  • What is the impact of research and development efforts in mobile application accessibility?
  • Have developers (collectively as a community) been doing a better or worse job in making their apps more accessible?
  • If thereâ€™s an improvement, has the improvement been steady, accelerating, or plateauing?
  • As a research community, should we be satisfied with our progress or should we do more?

Temporal Analysis: Privacy

  • Do apps tend to request fewer or more permissions over time?
  • Are developers becoming privacy-conscious or non-privacy-conscious over the years?
  • Do developers ask for more permissions to use in new features or included libraries?
